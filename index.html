Our Story

A Ministry Built
on Pure Worship

Eternal Praise Family is a community-based gospel music ministry founded by Pastor Attah-Nti Samuel, established with a divine mandate to train singers and instrumentalists, promote disciplined worship, preserve local and classic gospel music, and serve churches and the community through organized music ministry.

Our choir is not merely a performance group — we are ministers of the Most High God, committed to excellence, continuous growth, and the transformative power of worship. Based at All Nation Church, behind Page 4, off Kenyasi Road, Kumasi-Ashanti.

  • Excellence: We give our best to God in every rehearsal and ministration.
  • Community: We are a family bound by love, prayer, and shared purpose.
  • Spiritual Depth: Our music flows from a deep personal walk with God.
  • Growth: We invest in training, voice development, and music literacy.

Ministry Vision

Where We Are Going

Our vision is to be a choir that doesn't just sing songs but transforms lives through the power of anointed worship.

We aim to establish a fully equipped Music Academy, develop regional outreach concerts, and release our first studio worship album.

Spiritual Excellence

Every voice must be submitted to the Holy Spirit before it is trained in technique.

Music Education

Ongoing training in music theory, sight-reading, and vocal development for all members.

Outreach Ministry

Taking the sound of praise beyond our walls into hospitals, schools, and communities.

Media Production

Recording and releasing high-quality local and classic gospel worship content.

Membership

Praise Family

We welcome worshippers of all vocal ranges who have a heart for God, a love for music, and a commitment to excellence. No formal music training required — just a willing heart.

  • Vocal training & music literacy classes
  • Lifelong friendships & community
  • Opportunities to lead in worship
  • Performance at special programs & events
  • Access to choir library & resources
  • Registration: GHS 100–200 · Monthly dues: GHS 20
